An attempt to describe the ideas/image/space that came to my mind is: of increasingly small sections of each human being useful as a part of the current economic system while we continue to be a resource for it so our consciousness is increasingly stretched between ground and cloud so to speak. A state of the world where most of humanity exists completely outside the most powerful economy, occasionally finding an opportunity to draw down resources while this now separate economic system continues to take what it wants from the earth. The drawings are an architectural-apocalyptic-geometric analogy of this process/state: a dark landscape painfully tugged up by wires like mosquito’s proboscis, a sky only defined by sharp white lines, parts that resist the cling of the charcoal and break its continuity and softness. ‘Beings of sunshine and light’ - Haraway's phrase referring to tech has been echoing round my head for months.
